Dear Family and Friends

This letter is for my family and friends. You are the world to me. Without each and every one of you, i will be lost. You gave me a reason to live. You all are my reason to live. I will love every single one of you until i die. We will stand together and face all problems together. You fall, I fall. You rise, I rise. This bond that we have will never break.
