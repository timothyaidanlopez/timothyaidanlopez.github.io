Dear Future Special Someone

This is a letter for you, my love. Whoever you are, I want you to know that i will always love you. Even if we are apart, even if seas and mountains are between us, I will cross them all just to be with you. No matter how hard we fight, i assure you that we will never break. I vow to always stand by your side. I vow to be yours and only yours.
